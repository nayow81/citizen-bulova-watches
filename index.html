Eco-Drive Collection

In 1976, we invented the world’s first light-powered, analogue quartz watch that runs using only light as a power source. This technology, now known as ECO-DRIVE, can generate power from any light source – artificial, natural and even dim light – to keep watches running without ever replacing batteries.

Atomic Timekeeping Collection

We launched the world’s first multiband Atomic Timekeeping watch in 1993, and remain a pioneer of this field. Combined with our proprietary light-powered ECO-DRIVE technology, when owning a Citizen Atomic Timekeeping watch, you will get precise time powered by any light source.

Why Bulova?

Bulova's industry innovations have included everything from pioneering standardized production of watches to developing the first fully electronic watch with proprietary tuning fork-based technology, making it at the time the most accurate watch in the world. But our breakthroughs reached beyond the world of timekeeping alone.
